Blue Bay Bangladesh

Welcome to Blue Bay Bangladesh (BBB), one of the leading business houses in the country engaged in all kinds of maritime treading, indenting, consulting & industrial raw materials supplying (e.g. Steel Mills, Glass, and Ceramic Factory, etc.).

Founded in 2010, BBB, specifically to introduce the Bangladesh shipbuilding industry to the world. During this period, we gained the trust of our clients, evolving in different dimensions with a variety of business activities & have been nourishing commendable reputation & status both at home & abroad.

To our clients worldwide, BBB has always been and will remain a reliable, competent, efficient, and cooperative partner with valuable resources.

Co-operation with BBB proves to be a unique and delightful experience.
